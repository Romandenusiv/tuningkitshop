Poor kit

Poor kit

About 'Poor kit': A stage 1 modification can be added in isolation. This means that a true stage 1 modification part does not require any other engine modifications to get it to work. Whilst other mods can help to raise the power gains and realize the full potential of the mod they are not mandatory. These are at the bottom end of the tuning scene in terms of the overall benefits you will get.

Sport kit

A stage 3 modification is regarded by most as a track day modification. Like stage 2 mods they will also need other mods to support them but they are usually far from ideal for road use as we will explain. An aggressive cam profile will also move the power band right up the rev range and cause a very lumpy tick over making the car hard to drive in day to day traffic. A stage 3 race modified car will also need to be regularly overhauled and serviced
